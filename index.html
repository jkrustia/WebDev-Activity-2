Shrimp Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 10 cups of cold water
  • 2 medium carrots, quartered
  • 2 stalks celery, quartered
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 1 head garlic, halved
  • 2 lemons
  • 1/2 bunch parsley
  • 5 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 500g medium shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Hot sauce, to taste

Procedure

  1. Bring the 10 cups of cold water to a boil in a large pot. Add the quartered carrots, celery, onion, halved garlic, lemons, parsley, thyme, and bay leaves. Reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes to create a flavorful broth.

  2. Add the 500g of medium shrimp and 1 tablespoon of salt to the simmering broth. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the shrimp turn pink and are cooked through.

  3. Remove the shrimp from the pot using a slotted spoon and let them cool completely. Once cooled, peel and devein the shrimp.

  4. In a separate bowl, combine the 1 cup of ketchup, 1/4 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce to taste. Stir until well combined to create the cocktail sauce.

  5. Serve the chilled shrimp with the prepared cocktail sauce.

Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 500g beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28oz tomato sauce
  • 3oz tomato paste
  • 14oz crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 250g dry lasagna noodles
  • 15oz ricotta cheese
  • 24oz mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 4oz parmesan cheese, grated

Procedure

  1. Brown the beef in olive oil. Add onion, bell pepper, and garlic; cook until softened.

  2. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, oregano, parsley, garlic powder, salt, vinegar, and sugar. Simmer for 30 minutes.

  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.

  4. Spread 1/3 of the meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.

  5. Layer 1/2 of the noodles, 1/2 of the ricotta cheese, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese.

  6. Repeat layers.

  7. Top with remaining meat sauce and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

  8. Bake at 375Â°F for 25-30 minutes, or until bubbly and cheese is melted.

  9. Let it sit for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.

Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 250ml all purpose cream
  • 150g cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 teaspoon coffee grounds
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 10 pcs broas
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder

Procedure

  1. Combine all purpose cream, cream cheese, condensed milk, and vanilla. Whisk until smooth.

  2. Mix hot water, coffee, and sugar. Briefly soak broas in coffee mixture and quickly assemble in a container in layers with the cream mixture.

  3. Refrigerate overnight. Top with cocoa powder before serving.
